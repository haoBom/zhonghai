Zhonghai Wantai is a mixed ownership high-tech enterprise under CNOOC, focusing on the research and development of high-end equipment in the field of offshore oil and gas exploration and development.

As the first digital intelligent production base for the "Xuanji" system (rotary directional drilling and logging while drilling technology) in China, the company has overcome international leading technologies such as deep hole gun drilling and laser cladding, obtained API Q1 international certification, and built a full industry chain system for research and development design, precision machining, and final assembly testing.

Its core products have broken through the bottleneck of domestication of deep-sea oil and gas equipment, with technical indicators reaching the world's advanced level.

It is committed to becoming a one-stop solution provider for global marine engineering equipment and promoting the implementation of the national major equipment self-reliance strategy.
