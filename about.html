What we focuse on

The company focuses on high-end equipment manufacturing in the field of petroleum exploration and development. Its leading products include rotary directional drilling systems (Welleader), while drilling logging systems (Drilog), and open eye cable logging equipment. Its core technologies include deep hole drilling, gun drilling, laser cladding, and other difficult processing techniques, and its technical level is internationally leading.

Leading domestic and internationally renowned

As the first intelligent production line carrier of the "Xuanji" system in China, the company has an annual production capacity of over 100 sets of equipment and T4 level maintenance capability. It has five professional workshops, more than 200 precision CNC equipment, and has passed dual certifications of API Q1 and ISO. Its business covers the entire chain of precision component manufacturing, equipment assembly, testing and maintenance, and is committed to providing one-stop solutions for the entire lifecycle of oil and gas fields. The company is positioned as a "leading domestic and internationally renowned" marine engineering equipment service provider, focusing on promoting the localization of high-end petroleum machinery and equipment, serving CNOOC Group and the global market, with the goal of becoming a leading enterprise in the field of world oilfield exploration equipment.
